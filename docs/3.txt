Actually, Veit Loers wanted to be a painter. After his applications to art academies were unsuccessful, he studied art history and found himself after years of museum work in contemporary art, but as a curator. His exhibitions, with all their differences, have a penchant for the subversive, the grotesque and the occult both in a collective and individual dimension.
